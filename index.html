About Me

I have worked in the travel industry for 14 years and with 2 different companies. The first 12 years were spent with a major airline. An opportunity with a startup tech company that focused on corporate business travel presented itself to me, so I took it! It was difficult and so rewarding, all at the same time! Working for that company really began my curiosity and interest into the tech world!
